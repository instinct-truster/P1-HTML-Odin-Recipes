Pizza Crust

Looks Like Pizza's Back on the Menu, Boys

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Organic Ground Chicken
  • 3 Organic Free Range Eggs
  • 5 oz High Quality Pork Rinds, example 2 EPIC bags
  • 1 tablespoon Grass Fed Ghee or Butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon Sea Salt
  • 1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. In your food processor, crush the pork rinds to a fine powder. Set the pork rind powder aside.
  2. Melt the ghee on your stovetop.
  3. In your food processor, mix the raw ground chicken, melted ghee, eggs, sea salt and Italian seasoning until a smooth paste forms.
  4. Add the pork rind powder to the food processor along with the paste and blend. A thick, dense dough will be created.
  5. Transfer the dough on top of parchment paper and place it on a baking sheet.
  6. Form the dough into your preferred pizza shape. I made a 12-inch circle with mine, then started in the center and pushed the dough outward, thinning out the bottom and forming a thick crust. You could also do flatbread pizzas or thin crust if you prefer.
  7. Once you have formed your crust, bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 375 degrees.
  8. Top with pizza toppings (sauce, cheese, cooked meat, etc.) as you would with a regular naked crust. Put the crust back in the oven (with the toppings) for 10-15 minutes to re-heat the toppings and/or melt the cheese.
